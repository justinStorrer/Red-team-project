How to contact support

Sweet Release is commited to customer satisfaction and that includes our customer support. Because ghosts can't use phones, contacting support can be a bit more difficult. While you can see the instructions on this page, all these will be included in the Sweet Release package. To contact support, you must do the following.

  1. Visit our headquarters or one of our site locations
  2. Following the signs to our support section. A map is located in the lobby as well.
  3. Enter an unoccupied Ouija board booth where a trained professional team will constantly check if someone needs support.
  4. After you have made your presence known, follow the team member you notified to crystal ball booth where our staff can talk to you.
  5. Now you just need to talk to our support team about your issue an we will solve your problem as fast as we can.
